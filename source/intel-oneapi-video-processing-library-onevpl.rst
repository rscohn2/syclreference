Intel oneAPI Video Processing Library (oneVPL)
==============================================


Intel oneAPI Video Processing Library (oneVPL) provides cross platform
support and vendor independent support for accelerated video processing.
oneVPL is designed to simplify interacting with video content in
applications where accelerators with independent storage are leveraged.
